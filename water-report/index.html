Overview

The AZ Water Report domain provides a bounded, deterministic view of Arizona's water storage infrastructure, focusing on the Salt and Verde river systems managed by SRP. It demonstrates the Project Phoenix principles of data-anchored tools and multi-table logical composition.

Highlights

Deterministic Tools

Lookup and aggregation logic is anchored to frozen CSV substrates, not model memory.

Infrastructure Status

Real-time usability evaluation for boat ramps across the entire storage system.

Progressive Maturity

From factual retrieval (V1) to structured generative recommendations (V2).
