The Yield Formula

Y = ‚àè(1 - p_i)^n_i

Y is the probability of zero defects, p_i is the fault probability for component i, and n_i is the component count on a board.

SLSQP Optimization

  • Optimizer: scipy.optimize.minimize (SLSQP)
  • Bounds: 0.0 to max_fault_prob (default 0.05)
  • Objective: minimize squared error between predicted and observed yields
