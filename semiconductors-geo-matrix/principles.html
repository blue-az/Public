Core Principles Applied

Write-Then-Verify

Catalyst notes are written to disk, then explicitly read back via VerifyCatalystNote before being used downstream. SOP runs are logged and verified via VerifyCatalystSOP.

Anti-Hallucination Templates

Executive briefings use structured templates with stable fields: market segments, share percentages, source citations, and date-scoped snapshots. No fabricated figures.

Source Attribution

All market share observations carry a source field. SEC EDGAR backs financial metrics. Stooq provides price history. Share data mapped from authoritative analyst sources.

Atomic Operations

Each tool owns exactly one query or action. GetTopMarketShareLeaders ranks. GetMarketShareTrend trends. CompareCompanies compares. No tool does two things.

Variation Strategy

8 configurations from core analysis (V1) to interactive dashboards (V8). Each variation adds a complete new capability layer — catalyst analysis, SOP orchestration, or visualization.

Data Integrity Rules

  • Never report market share without a source family and date.
  • All share percentages are snapshot-based — not projections.
  • Financial metrics come from SEC company facts (not estimated).
  • Alias-aware company lookup prevents duplicate or missed entities.
  • Event catalyst windows use exact event dates, not approximations.
  • PSI/statistical methods are not applied to market share — it is authoritative.
