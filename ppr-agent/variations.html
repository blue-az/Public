Variation 1: Complete (6 tools)

28 tasks | 20 rules

Full-featured analytical capability covering all aspects of PPR data.

Tools

  • GetTopDevicesByImplants - Rankings by volume
  • GetMarketShareTrend - Market share over time
  • CompareCompanies - Multi-company analysis
  • GetDeviceLongevity - Long-term product tracking
  • GetYearOverYearGrowth - Growth trend analysis
  • SearchDevices - Text search

Use Cases

  • Comprehensive market analysis
  • Strategic planning requiring multiple perspectives
  • Research requiring broad analytical capabilities
