Domain Overview

PPR_Agent is an automated system for extracting and analyzing cardiac rhythm management (CRM) device data from Product Performance Reports (PPRs) published by Abbott, Boston Scientific, and Medtronic.

The system covers 17 years of data (2008-2024) with comprehensive market share analysis, competitive intelligence, and trend forecasting capabilities.
