Domain Overview

This domain delivers deterministic company-performance intelligence for Amkor Technology (AMKR), the world's second-largest OSAT (Outsourced Semiconductor Assembly and Test) provider. The system operates as a Glass Box expert agent — every plan is visible and interruptible before execution.

The domain is structured around four analytical variations that teach progressively: from operational snapshots (V1) to compact KPIs (V2) to time-series trend analysis (V3) to readiness-gated advisory hypothesis synthesis (V4).

Primary strategic thesis: Amkor's ~$2B Arizona advanced packaging facility, anchored by Apple, positions AMKR as the leading US-domestic OSAT provider at the moment AI and HPC chip packaging demand is inflecting.

Domain Context

OSAT Landscape

Most OSAT direct peers (JCET, Powertech, KYEC) are Taiwan/China-listed with limited SEC data. ASX (ASE Technology) is the only US-accessible OSAT peer — tracked as a foreign private issuer via IFRS-full XBRL.

Arizona Facility

~$2B advanced packaging facility in Peoria, AZ with Apple as anchor customer. Opened 2024–2025. Eligible for CHIPS Act incentive funding. This is the dominant near-term strategic story.

AI Packaging Demand

AMKR benefits from rising 2.5D/3D chip stacking (HBM, CoWoS-adjacent) demand driven by GPU and AI accelerator production ramps. Advanced packaging is a structurally higher-margin product mix shift.

Seasonality

AMKR revenue peaks Q4 due to consumer electronics build cycles. Year-over-year comparisons require seasonality normalization — the domain handles this via the GetRevenueTrend and GetMultiMetricSummary tools.
