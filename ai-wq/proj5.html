Overview

Training Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) for image generation. This project covers the adversarial training paradigm with generator and discriminator networks competing to produce realistic images.

Key Techniques

  • Adversarial Training - Minimax game between generator and discriminator
  • Generator Network - Transforms latent vectors to images via transposed convolutions
  • Discriminator Network - Binary classifier distinguishing real from fake images
  • Training Stability - Techniques for stable GAN training (batch norm, LeakyReLU)
