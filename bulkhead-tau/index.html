The market is shipping rafts. Bulkhead τ ships vessels.

Purpose

Bulkhead τ is the public release line for a system that does not try to make AI smarter. It tries to make AI governable. The intelligence sits inside a watertight shell, bounded above by an explicit Charter and below by an explicit Logbook. Inputs arrive as fuel; outputs leave as repeatable insights; nothing leaks.

Where the rest of the AI tooling space is building thin wrappers around language models — thousands of rafts, fast to build and quick to capsize — Bulkhead τ is the engineered alternative: a deterministic substrate that survives contact with real domains.

The Three-Tier Seal

Bulkhead τ sits in the middle of a bounded architecture. It is not the whole system. It is the watertight core inside a larger discipline.

Upper Boundary — PBC

The Charter. Component-Based Governance documents (.pbc.md) define mission, scope, and routing constraints. The engine room obeys the Charter. When the documentation changes, the system changes course — not the other way around.

Core — Bulkhead τ

The watertight engine room. Intakes fuel (data), executes physics (deterministic substrates plus governed agent use), and delivers thrust (validated outputs). Hallucination seepage is contained, not tolerated.

Lower Boundary — GT‑KB

The Logbook. Every vibration, every turn of the screw, every routing decision feeds the ground-truth knowledge base. The system is auditable because the Logbook is non-optional.

Why This Exists

Determinism

Real execution and validation live in explicit domain logic, not in prompt behavior. The substrate is the authority; the model is the interface.

Auditability

Claims, artifacts, and trust boundaries are inspectable. The Logbook records what happened; the Charter records what was supposed to happen; the gap between the two is where the work is.

Bounded Discipline

The system does not try to do everything. It is strictly bounded by two adjacent disciplines — PBC above, GT‑KB below — so failures localize to a tier instead of smearing across the stack.

Useful Standards

Patterns other operators can adapt without copying the full consulting process. The papers below are part of that contract.

What This Is Not

  • Not a chatbot layer. Bulkhead τ does not wrap a model with prompts; it wraps a model with structure.
  • Not a benchmark suite. The papers are field evidence from production-shape lanes, not synthetic leaderboards.
  • Not a replacement for engineering judgment. The Charter is written by humans; the Logbook is read by humans; the engine room exists to make both possible at scale.
  • Not a magic claim. The scope is bounded: well-defined domain tasks where deterministic substrates exist. Outside that scope, Bulkhead τ does not pretend to be load-bearing.
