Purpose

Bulkhead τ is the public release line for a system that does not try to make AI smarter. It tries to make AI governable. The intelligence sits inside a watertight shell, bounded above by an explicit Charter (PBC) and below by an explicit Logbook (GT-KB). Inputs arrive as fuel; outputs leave as repeatable insights; nothing leaks.

Where the rest of the AI tooling space is building thin wrappers around language models — thousands of rafts, fast to build and quick to capsize — Bulkhead τ is the engineered alternative: a deterministic substrate that survives contact with real domains.

The Three-Tier Seal

Upper Boundary — PBC

The Charter. Component-Based Governance documents (.pbc.md) define mission, scope, and routing constraints. The engine room obeys the Charter.

Core — Bulkhead τ

The watertight engine room. Intakes data, executes physics via deterministic substrates, and delivers validated thrust. Hallucination seepage is contained.

Lower Boundary — GT-KB

The Logbook. Every vibration and routing decision feeds the ground-truth knowledge base. The system is auditable because the Logbook is non-optional.
