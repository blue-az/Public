BULKHEAD τ: ROUTER

Validate incoming datagrams against the Data Boundary. Route poisoned packets to Quarantine to prevent critical system breaches. Do not let corrupted data breach the subsystems.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Each packet on the left has fields: TYPE, SUM, PRIO, TTL, DATA.
  • If a packet is VALID, route it to its matching subsystem lane.
  • If a packet violates ANY schema rule, route it to QUARANTINE.
  • Safe Strategy: Quarantining a valid packet only costs score (-25 throughput). Routing a poisoned packet to a subsystem deals a massive -25% Hull Integrity hit. When in doubt, quarantine!
