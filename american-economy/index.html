What This Domain Is For

This domain is designed for rapid macro review, not heavy econometric modeling. The focus is clear visuals, practical signal tracking, and understandable U.S.-to-world context.

Current focus: Debt and deficit are now explicit first-class visuals in both static and interactive dashboards.

Coverage Areas

  • Growth: U.S. and world GDP level and growth comparisons.
  • Inflation and labor: CPI and unemployment trends.
  • Fiscal: Government debt-to-GDP and deficit-to-GDP.
  • External linkage: Trade openness and cross-country matrix snapshots.
  • Interactive review: Plotly dashboards and research packs for drill-down.
