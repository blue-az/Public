Greeks Visualization

  • Delta (Δ): Call (green, 0→1) and put (red, 0→-1) price sensitivity

  • Theta (Θ): Coral dotted - time decay (negative), highest ATM
  • Vega (ν): Purple dash-dot - volatility sensitivity, highest ATM
