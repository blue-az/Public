V1: Recipe Lookup (10 tools)

Read-only tools for searching and browsing recipes. No data modification.

  • List categories and recipes
  • Search by title, cuisine, or ingredient
  • Get recipe details and ingredients
  • Access cooking technique references

Key constraint: V1 is completely read-only.

V2: Meal Planning (+4 = 14 tools)

Add meal planning and shopping list generation.

  • scale_recipe_servings - Adjust ingredient quantities
  • create_meal_plan - Create weekly plans
  • get_meal_plan - Retrieve plan details
  • generate_shopping_list - Aggregate ingredients

V3: Pantry & Discovery (+4 = 18 tools)

Find recipes based on available ingredients and get substitution suggestions.

  • find_recipes_by_pantry - What can I make?
  • suggest_substitution - Ingredient alternatives
  • list_common_ingredients - Popular ingredients
  • find_similar_recipes - Related dishes

V4: Personalization (+8 = 26 tools)

Track cooking journey and personalize the experience.

  • add_favorite / list_favorites - Save preferred recipes
  • log_cook_event / get_cook_history - Cooking log
  • add_recipe_note / get_recipe_notes - Personal notes
  • rate_recipe / list_top_rated - 5-star ratings
